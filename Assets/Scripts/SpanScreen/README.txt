Download AutoIt3
----------------
http://www.autoitscript.com/site/autoit/downloads/

How to use the script
---------------------
- Move script.bat and max.au3 on your desktop
- Move the shortcut on the desktop and edit the its link to be script.bat
- You may change keybindings (default is CTRL + ALT + W)

Use
---
Move your cursor on the maximize button and press CTRL + ALT + W
